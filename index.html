About

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Statistics at the University of Florida. My work focuses on modeling high-dimensional data with network structure — spanning Bayesian VAR, multi-layer Gaussian graphical models, and spline-based time-varying models — and applying deep learning to time series and computer vision problems.

Research Experience

  • Spline-Based Multi-Layer GGM (2024–2025) — Sparse B-spline expansions for regression coefficients and precision matrices across groups; scalable estimation and interpretable networks.
  • Fused Multi-Layer GGM (2023–2024) — Joint inference with group + fused lasso; alternating block optimization with split Bregman updates.
  • Bayesian Tree-Rank VAR (2020–2022) — Tree-rank prior + Gibbs sampling; improved structure learning on fMRI data.
  • Causal Effect Detection (2017) — High-dimensional simulations; heterogeneity detection via PALM/GLM trees.
